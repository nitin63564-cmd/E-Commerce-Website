Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions our customers ask

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping delivers within 1-2 business days. We also offer same-day delivery in select cities. Free shipping is available on all orders over $50.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply return the item in its original condition for a full refund. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly on the carrier's website.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, UPI, and Cash on Delivery in select areas. All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination (typically 7-15 business days). Additional customs fees may apply depending on your country's regulations.
